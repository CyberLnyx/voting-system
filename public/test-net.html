Must Read

After you've entered the email you submitted for your representatives, click Verify Email to proceed.

The following are the possible scenario you might encounter:

1. Email does not exist.
2. Verification code sent to your email

If you get Email does not exist. Immediately contact your representative and submit your email to them.

If you get Verification code sent to your email. Check your email to confirm if you get the Verification code.
If you get the Verification code, then, no further action is required from you. However, If you didn't get the verification code, Immediately contact your representative, submit your email and add the text I didn't get the verification code.
